Ingredients:
 - 2 eggs, medium
 - 1/2 onion, chopped
 - 1/2 tomato, chopped
 - 2 chilli peppers, finely chopped
 - 1 teaspoon chili powder
 - 1 teaspoon ground oregano
 - 1 teaspoon salt


Directions:
 1. Mix eggs, tomato, onion and chilli pepper, salt and oregano into a bol and beat.
 2. Add onto pan and cook on high heat for 3 minutes. Sprinkle chilli powder on top of the omelette.
 3. Simmer over medium heat for two minutes.
 4. Flip omelette.
 5. Simmer over medium heat for two minutes.
